about us

ORGANISATION HISTORY

Thusani Foundation care is a team of devoted individuals and volunteers who are committed to performing services to our communities. We have offered services to our communities at no cost and on various scales and We offer services that have proved to be beneficial to our communities and appreciated by our communities on a larger scale. We have seen a huge growth in our organisation since its inception and with positive feedback on our impact within our communities

contact us

BACKGROUND

Thusani Foundation Care is a community-basedorganisation focused on providing opportunities to those that have less access, mainly women, children and disabled individuals in sports, information and basic needs. It also serves as a vehicle to empower and develop more individuals in its area of operations. Our enterprise employs relevantly qualified and suitable candidates who have the necessary skills in all our service offering and programme suite field stated above.

framework

our vission

Thusani Foundation Care want children and young people to grow up in the safe environment, to be strong, self-sufficient and happy adults that in the end can contribute positively in the society. Thusani Foundation care believes that every child should have the chance to thrive and be the best they can be. We are committed to being there for the children across communities when they need us most.

contact us

our mission

Thusani Foundation Care’s mission is to break the cycle of vulnerable people in our community by loving them as if they were our own and seeking to promote health, literacy, education, and empowerment. We emphasize faith-based quality care, education, and treatment

our values

SAFETY

Plan safety into every aspect of our programmes and don’t deviate from that plan. Strive for zero incidents.

INTEGRITY

Our organisation will include the highest level of honesty, ethics, and moral correctness. We will not compromise volunteers and management, care receivers, or our organisation.

RESPECTS

Ensuring respects circulates between volunteers, management, community, third party, principles, laws, environment, regulations, and ethics.

TEAMWORK

We aim to work together as a team along with our community to deliver better service, we also collaborate with third parties like, technical and security specialists.

INNOVATION

As the world evolves with technology, we put in initiatives with our management, technical and maintenance teams to improve our future and current programmes.

key to success

• Excellence in fulfilling, promises, reliability and trustworthiness within agreed principles.

• Utilization of high-level expertise in the execution of tasks.

• Programmes back-up.

• Employment of ad-hoc professionals to achieve goals.

• On-going programme and service training.

our strategy

• To create and develop an organisation that will be able to compete with major players in the selected industries.

• To create and develop organisational models that will ensure growth, sustainability and maximum capitalization on the opportunities provided by the public and private sector.

• To contribute to the socio-economic objectives of the country by creating an expansion through optimization, therefore increasing the demand for skilled and un-skilled labour.

• To continue to forge strategic partnership with relevant partners in our communities
